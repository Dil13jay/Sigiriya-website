The Rise of Sigiriya

Discover how King Kashyapa transformed a towering rock into a magnificent fortress and royal palace in the 5th century. Sigiriya stands today as a symbol of ancient power, creativity, and engineering brilliance.

Frescoes, Gardens, and the Mirror Wall

Walk through beautifully designed water gardens, marvel at ancient frescoes high up the rock, and read centuries-old writings on the Mirror Wall. Sigiriya is a masterpiece of ancient urban design and creativity.

About Me

As a first-year computing undergraduate of NSBM Green University, I have developed a website dedicated to the historical marvel of Sigiriya, one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic landmarks. Leveraging my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, I created an interactive and visually engaging platform that highlights the cultural significance and breathtaking beauty of Sigiriya. This project aims to inspire curiosity and encourage more visitors to explore this ancient rock fortress, while showcasing the rich heritage Sri Lanka has to offer.
