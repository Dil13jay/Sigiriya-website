About Me

Developer of Sigiriya Wonders

Dilmi Jayaweera

I am a first-year computing undergraduate at NSBM Green University, and the creator of this individual project — Sigiriya Wonders. This website was developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to showcase the heritage, history, and architectural beauty of Sigiriya, one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic landmarks.

My goal is to use modern web technologies to promote Sri Lanka’s tourism and educate users on the historical and cultural significance of this ancient site.
